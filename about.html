Trunk of Colors

Paint your soul green

I created Trunk of Colors as a place to slow down, breathe, and just be—without the weight of pressure or the need for perfection.

To me, art has never been about being "good" or "talented." It’s about showing up. It’s about letting color speak when words feel a little too heavy. It’s about finding a quiet calm in the brushstrokes and a genuine joy in the mess.

The Journey

What started as my own quiet escape into color has grown into this studio. Now, I host therapeutic sessions and workshops to help others find that same sense of peace.

No Rules

Whether you’re holding a brush for the first time or finding your way back after years away, you’re welcome here exactly as you are.

At Trunk of Colors, I’m not here to teach you how to make perfect art.
I’m here to help you feel good while you make it.

About the Founder

Pooja Jalan

I’ve always believed that creativity isn’t a "gift" reserved for a lucky few, it’s just a way of feeling. As a self-taught artist, I’ve learned that the brush is less about technique and more about finding a way to breathe.

For me, Trunk of Colors is a personal invitation to slow down. In a world that rarely pauses, art is how I process my emotions and return to myself. My process is intuitive, a little messy, and deeply human.

I’m here for the presence, the play, and that quiet confidence that only grows when you finally give yourself permission to create freely.
